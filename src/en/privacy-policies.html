BLOWFISH Privacy Policy

The purpose of this Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") is to inform the Users of the comprehensive scope of protection of their personal data recorded in our files; records; banks or databases; or other technical data-processing tools implemented by TOOIT SH or BLOWFISH.

BLOWFISH has created this Privacy Policy to demonstrate our commitment to the privacy of Users and visitors on our website (the "Website") and within the services (the "Services") delivered by BLOWFISH. This Privacy Policy describes our data-collection and data-sharing practices. By visiting the Website or using the Services, you agree to the collection and use of the information in the manner described below.

If you have questions about this policy regarding the use of information, you can send an email to legal@blowfish.la

Use of information

BLOWFISH uses the information we collect to configure the Services for Users and to improve the Website. We may also use the information to contact Users and visitors to discuss their interest in the Website and Services/Service Offerings, or to send users and visitors information about BLOWFISH and/or business partners, such as new features, promotions and events. In order not to receive such information, an option to cancel the subscription (“Opt-Out”) is included in every communication. We may also email Users about information regarding updates to the Services or the Website.

All financial and billing information collected through the Website is used solely to verify the qualifications of prospective users and invoice the Services. This billing information is not used by BLOWFISH for marketing or promotional purposes. Other third parties, such as content providers, may provide content on the Website or Services, but are not authorized to collect information; nor is BLOWFISH to share any User or visitor information with these parties.

Information Collected

Registration Information and Billing: When a Website visitor registers to become a User of the Services or ask for more information about Services, we require visitors to provide contact information such as (for example) your name, email address, telephone number, age, gender, company name, billing information such as billing address and phone number. We may also ask visitors, Users or potential users of the Services for additional personal information, such as their professional experience, technical background and plans to use the Service. Users and visitors may choose not to provide this additional information by not entering it when asked. However, failure to provide certain requested information may affect the User's or visitor's experience and the ability to access the Website and/or Services.

Data Logging and Monitoring Activity: Blowfish collects certain information from Website visitors and Users of the Services, such as Internet addresses, types of navigation, domain references, temporary navigation data and use as well as specific webpages that the visitor has requested. This information is recorded to provide a better user experience, diagnose technical problems, marketing purposes and with the aim of improving Website and Services management in order to constantly improve the quality of the offerings. We may also track and analyze non-identifying activities and add statistical usage information and volume of visitors and users and provide such information to third parties.

Cookies: Cookies are files that Web browsers place on a computer’s hard-drive. BLOWFISH uses cookies to notify if users and visitors have previously visited the Website or the Services. BLOWFISH uses session-based and persistent cookies. Session cookies only exist during a session and disappear when you close the browser. Persistent cookies remain on your computer after you close the browser or shut down the computer. If the cookie is rejected, access to and use of the Services may be denied or otherwise limited.

Stored Data

Users of the Services will use the Services to store data. This data may be viewed or accessed by BLOWFISH only for the purpose of resolving a problem, support or suspected violation of the Terms and Conditions for the Website and Services, or as required by Law. Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality and security of their user registration, login and password. Except as stated in this Privacy Policy, BLOWFISH will not examine, share, distribute, print or make reference to any data stored by Users on the Website or the Services.

Exchange of information

BLOWFISH may share aggregate demographic information with partners and advertisers. This is not linked to any personal information that can identify an individual person.

BLOWFISH uses a third-party service provider to process credit-card billing. This service provider is authorized to store, retain or use billing information only for the sole purpose of processing credit cards on behalf of BLOWFISH. BLOWFISH can partner with other parties to provide specific services. If a user registers for these services, we will share the names or other contact information that is necessary for the third party to provide these services. These third parties may not use personally identifiable information except for the purpose of providing these services. BLOWFISH is responsible for the privacy of the information transferred to third parties.

We may arbitrarily provide user information to BLOWFISH, its associates and its affiliates in order to provide those users with the Services. Each of these entities will comply with the policies described in this Privacy Policy. In addition, if BLOWFISH’s shares or assets, its affiliates, joint-ventures or any combination thereof are acquired by another entity, such successor may be provided with some or all of BLOWFISH’s users and visitors personal information and hosted data. In such a case, the successor will handle personal information and hosted data in the same manner as provided in this Privacy Policy. We may be required, under special circumstances, to disclose personal information in response to a legal request by the public authorities, law enforcement, and security agencies of the Argentine Nation.

BLOWFISH will not share, sell or rent personal or personally identifiable User or visitor information with anyone outside of BLOWFISH, without the prior permission of this User or visitor, or ordered by a court of Law.

Security

BLOWFISH uses the appropriate administrative, technical and physical security measures to protect the information and data of our Users and visitors against loss, misuse and alteration. We use industry-standard practices to help prevent unauthorized use, access or alteration of visitor and User information and hosted data. These practices include the appropriate use of firewalls, SSL encryption, limiting the storage of financial information to a third party PCI compliant provider, system redundancies, and joint location in a 24/7 secure and controlled environment.

Exclusion / Opt-out

When visitors or Users send requests for more information or Services to us, they may choose not to enter certain fields. However, such a decision may affect the ability to use portions of the Website and Services. If you receive marketing messages or similar emails from us and wish to opt out of receiving such messages, you can simply follow the exclusion procedure specified in that email.

Updating contact information

We encourage our visitors and users to update and edit their contact information and keep it updated. Users may change or update their contact information at any time during the use of the Service.

Notification of changes

BLOWFISH reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy. BLOWFISH will provide notice of material changes to this Privacy Policy through the Website at least ten (10) business days before the changes take effect. If we make significant changes to our privacy practices that do not affect visitor or user information already stored in our database, we will post a prominent notice on the Website or Services notifying Users of such change. In some cases when publish the notice, we will also send an email to users who have chosen to receive communications from us to notify them of changes in our Privacy Policy.
